# NeuroNomad
Your AI-powered adventure travel companion 🌍
euroNomad is an AI-powered travel companion that helps adventure lovers discover hidden gems, personalized itineraries, and unique experiences based on their interests and travel style.
 AI-powered itinerary generation
 Hidden gem discovery engine
 Interactive map integration
 Mobile app (iOS & Android)
 Offline mode with downloadable itineraries
 Social trip sharing & collaboration
 AR-powered landmark exploration
 Multi-language support (20+ languages)
 Budget tracker & expense planner
 Integration with booking platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com)
